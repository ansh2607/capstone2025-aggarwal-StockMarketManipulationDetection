<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Item 8.01 Other Events.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">As previously disclosed, in April
2017 Plug Power Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) issued a warrant to Amazon.com NV Investment Holdings LLC to acquire up to
55,286,696 shares of the Company&#8217;s common stock. The vesting of the warrant is linked to payments made by Amazon.com,
Inc. and its affiliates to the Company pursuant to existing commercial agreements.&#160; As of November 2, 2020, 34,917,912
shares had vested under the warrant at an exercise price of $1.1893 per share based on total cumulative spending with the
Company in excess of $200 million.&#160; Under the terms of the warrant, at the same time the exercise price for the unvested
20,368,784 shares was set at $13.81005 per share. On December 31, 2020, the Company waived the remaining vesting conditions
under the warrant, which resulted in the vesting of the remaining 20,368,784 unvested shares under the warrant.&#160; This
vesting is expected to result in a substantial one-time non-cash charge in the quarter ended December 31, 2020, to eliminate
the need to recognize future quarterly non-cash&#160;charges for this warrant and to simplify the Company&#8217;s financial
reporting going forward.&#160; The details of the warrant and vesting will be described in the Company&#8217;s annual report
on Form 10-K for the year ending December 31, 2020 to be filed by the Company with the Securities and Exchange Commission
(the &#8220;SEC&#8221;).</p>
