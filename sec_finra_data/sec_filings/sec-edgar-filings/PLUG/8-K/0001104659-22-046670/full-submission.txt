<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Item&#160;5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election
of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">(d)&#160; The Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of Plug
Power Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) approved, effective as of April&#160;14, 2022, an increase in the size of the Board from ten directors
to twelve directors and appointed Jean Bua and Kavita Mahtani as directors of the Company to fill the newly created vacancies. Ms.&#160;Bua
and Ms.&#160;Mahtani were also appointed to the audit committee of the Company&#8217;s Board, with Ms.&#160;Bua appointed as the chair
of the audit committee.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Jean Bua has been designated as a Class&#160;II director to serve
until the Company&#8217;s 2022 annual meeting of the stockholders or until her successor has been duly elected and qualified, or
until her earlier death, removal or resignation. The Board has determined that Ms.&#160;Bua is an independent director as defined in
the NASDAQ Stock Market listing standards and applicable Securities and Exchange Commission (&#8220;SEC&#8221;) rules&#160;and
regulations. <span style="background-color: white">In addition, the Board has determined that Ms.&#160;Bua qualifies as an
&#8220;audit committee financial expert&#8221; as defined in the applicable rules&#160;of the SEC.</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Jean Bua&#160;currently is the Executive Vice President, Chief Financial
Officer, Chief Accounting Officer and Treasurer of NetScout Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ: NTCT), a provider of real-time operational intelligence
and performance analytics for service assurance and cyber security solutions. Prior to NetScout, Ms.&#160;Bua served as Executive Vice
President, Finance &amp; Treasurer of American Tower Corporation, an operator of wireless and broadcast communications infrastructure,
and spent nine&#160;years at Iron Mountain, Inc., an information management services company, concluding as Senior Vice President, Chief
Accounting Officer and Worldwide Controller. She also previously held senior positions at Duracraft Corp. and Keithley Instruments and
was a management consultant at Ernst &amp; Young LLP and an auditor at KPMG LLP. She has led all global financial operations, including
M&amp;A analysis, acquisition integration, capital market strategy, financial planning and analysis, international tax, financial systems
and compliance for high-growth, transformative public companies. Ms.&#160;Bua served as a Board member and Audit Committee Chair for Coresite
Realty until its acquisition at the end of 2021. Ms.&#160;Bua earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, summa cum laude,
from Bryant College and an M.B.A. from the University of Rhode Island.&#160;</p>

provider of turnkey hydrogen solutions for the global green hydrogen economy, announced today the appointment of two new members to the

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Jean Bua&nbsp;currently is the Executive Vice President, Chief Financial
Officer, Chief Accounting Officer and Treasurer of NetScout Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ: NTCT), a provider of real-time operational intelligence
and performance analytics for service assurance and cyber security solutions. Prior to NetScout, Ms.&nbsp;Bua served as Executive Vice
President, Finance &amp; Treasurer of American Tower Corporation, an operator of wireless and broadcast communications infrastructure,
and spent nine&nbsp;years at Iron Mountain, Inc., an information management services company, concluding as Senior Vice President, Chief
Accounting Officer and Worldwide Controller. She also previously held senior positions at Duracraft Corp. and Keithley Instruments and
was a management consultant at Ernst &amp; Young LLP and an auditor at KPMG LLP. She has led all global financial operations, including
M&amp;A analysis, acquisition integration, capital market strategy, financial planning and analysis, international tax, financial systems
and compliance for high-growth, transformative public companies. Ms.&nbsp;Bua served as a Board member and Audit Committee Chair for Coresite
Realty until its acquisition at the end of 2021. Ms.&nbsp;Bua earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, summa cum laude,
from Bryant College and an M.B.A. from the University of Rhode Island.</P>

drives growth and M&amp;A strategy, as well as restructuring and re-engineering efforts alongside the CEO. Prior to joining HSBC, Mahtani

asset and liability management, chief financial officer, global corporate and investment banking, and managing director &ndash; global
head of financial planning and analysis, among others. Mahtani has also held roles with Morgan Stanley and Merrill Lynch &amp; Company,
