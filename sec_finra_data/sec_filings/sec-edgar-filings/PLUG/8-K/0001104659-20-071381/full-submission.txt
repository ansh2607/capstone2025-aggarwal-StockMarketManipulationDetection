<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">Plug Power Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;)
held its 2020 annual meeting of stockholders (the &#8220;Annual Meeting&#8221;) in a virtual meeting format via live audio webcast
on June 5, 2010. At the Annual Meeting, the Company&#8217;s stockholders voted upon the following three matters:</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">1.&#160; The election of Lucas P. Schneider
and Jonathan Silver as Class&#160;III Directors, each to hold office until the Company&#8217;s 2023 annual meeting of stockholders
