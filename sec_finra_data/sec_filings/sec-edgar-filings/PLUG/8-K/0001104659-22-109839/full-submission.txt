<P STYLE="margin: 0; text-align: right"><B>Exhibit 99.1</B></P>

<P STYLE="margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 14pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Plug, Olin Corporation Launch Joint Venture
for<BR>
15-Ton-Per-Day Hydrogen Plant</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>Plug expects to produce 1,000 tons per day of
liquid hydrogen by 2028.</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>LATHAM, N.Y., Oct.&nbsp;19, 2022</B> &mdash;
<U>Plug Power Inc.</U> (NASDAQ: PLUG), a leading provider of turnkey hydrogen solutions for the global green hydrogen economy, and <U>Olin
Corporation</U> (NYSE: OLN), a leading vertically integrated chlor alkali producer and marketer, announced today the launch of a joint
venture to  begin with the construction of a 15-ton-per-day hydrogen plant in St. Gabriel, Louisiana.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">The joint venture, named Hidrogenii, will support
reliability of supply and speed to market for green hydrogen throughout North America, setting the foundation for broader collaboration
between the two companies.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">The partnership brings together
Plug, the company behind the end-to-end green hydrogen ecosystem, and Olin, North America&rsquo;s largest producer of electrolytic hydrogen.
Plug will be the exclusive marketer of the joint venture&rsquo;s hydrogen and provide logistical support for delivery, while Olin will
provide reliable hydrogen supply and operational expertise.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&ldquo;Plug is working rapidly
to meet the increasing demand of green hydrogen in North America and in the rest of the world,&rdquo; Andy Marsh, CEO of Plug, said.
 &ldquo;By partnering with like-minded companies, such as Olin, we&rsquo;ll make the transition to hydrogen easy and affordable.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">The plant will benefit from state
and local tax subsidies. The construction of the plant will create 160 jobs in 2022 and 215 jobs in 2023. The plant is then expected
to create more than 25 permanent full-time jobs in Louisiana.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&ldquo;The Hidrogenii venture
advances our commitment to transform Olin&rsquo;s hydrogen business into a value stream as we seek to grow the value of our 150,000 metric
tons of annual hydrogen production capacity,&rdquo; Scott Sutton, Olin Chairman, President, and Chief Executive Officer, said. &ldquo;We
are excited to partner with Plug, a leader in sustainable hydrogen, to serve the growing demand for green hydrogen.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">The Louisiana plant joins Plug&rsquo;s
growing national network of hydrogen plants in various planning and construction phases in New York, Tennessee, Georgia, Texas and California.
By 2025, Plug expects to produce 500 tons per day of liquid green hydrogen, which will prevent 4.3 million metric tons of carbon dioxide
emissions in North America. By 2028, Plug expects to produce 1,000 tons per day of liquid green hydrogen.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>About Plug</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Plug is building an end-to-end green hydrogen
ecosystem, from production, storage and delivery to energy generation, to help its customers meet their business goals and decarbonize
the economy. In creating the first commercially viable market for hydrogen fuel cell technology, the company has deployed more than 50,000
fuel cell systems and over 165 fueling stations, more than anyone else in the world, and is the largest buyer of liquid hydrogen. With
plans to build and operate a green hydrogen highway across North America and Europe, Plug is building a state-of-the-art Gigafactory
to produce electrolyzers and fuel cells and multiple green hydrogen production plants that will yield 500 tons of liquid green hydrogen
daily by 2025. Plug will deliver its green hydrogen solutions directly to its customers and through joint venture partners into multiple
environments, including material handling, e-mobility, power generation, and industrial applications. For more information, visit www.plugpower.com.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
