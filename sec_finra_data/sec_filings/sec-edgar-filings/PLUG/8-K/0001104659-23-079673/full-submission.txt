<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;<i>(a)&#160;Departure of Jean A. Bua and Jonathan M. Silver</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On July&#160;5, 2023, Jean A. Bua, a Class&#160;II director of the
Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of Plug Power Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), and Chair of the Board&#8217;s Audit Committee,
notified the Company of her decision to resign from the Board effective immediately. Ms.&#160;Bua has served on the Board since April&#160;2022.
Ms.&#160;Bua&#8217;s decision to resign from the Board is solely for personal reasons and not the result of a disagreement with the Company
on any matter relating to the Company&#8217;s operations, policies or practices.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On July&#160;10, 2023, Jonathan M. Silver, a Class&#160;III
director of the Board and member of the Board&#8217;s Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee and Regulatory Affairs
Committee, notified the Company of his decision to resign from the Board effective immediately. Mr.&#160;Silver has served on the
Board since June&#160;2018. Mr.&#160;Silver&#8217;s decision to resign from the Board is solely for personal and business reasons
and he has not advised the Company of a disagreement with the Company on any matter relating to the Company&#8217;s
operations, policies or practices. Following his resignation, Mr.&#160;Silver will serve as a consultant to the Company for a period
of 12 months and has entered into a consulting agreement with the Company, effective as of July&#160;10, 2023.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><span style="background-color: white">In connection with Mr.&#160;Silver&#8217;s
resignation, the Board approved the appointment of current Board member, </span>Gary K. Willis, as a member of the Board&#8217;s Nominating
and Corporate Governance Committee, effective immediately. Following Mr.&#160;Willis&#8217; appointment, the members of the Nominating
and Corporate Governance Committee are <span style="background-color: white">Maureen O. Helmer and </span>Gary K. Willis.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The Company wishes to express its gratitude to Ms.&#160;Bua and Mr.&#160;Silver
for each of their contributions to the Board.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>(d)&#160;Appointment of New Directors</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On July&#160;10, 2023, the Board appointed Patrick Joggerst and Mark
J. Bonney as directors of the Company to fill the vacancies created by the departure by Ms.&#160;Bua and Mr.&#160;Silver. In addition,
Mr.&#160;Bonney will replace Ms.&#160;Bua as the Chair of the Audit Committee of the Board effective immediately.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><span style="text-decoration: underline">Patrick Joggerst</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Patrick Joggerst has been designated as a Class&#160;III director to
serve until the Company&#8217;s 2026 annual meeting of the stockholders or until his successor has been duly elected and qualified, or
until his earlier death, removal or resignation. The Board has determined that Mr.&#160;Joggerst is an independent director as defined
in the NASDAQ Stock Market listing standards and applicable Securities and Exchange Commission (&#8220;SEC&#8221;) rules&#160;and regulations.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Patrick Joggerst is currently the Founder and Principal at J2 Consulting,
a management consulting practice focusing on organization transformation and sales acceleration. From January&#160;2018 until November&#160;2021,
Mr.&#160;Joggerst served as Chief Marketing Officer and Executive Vice President of Business Development at Ribbon Communications Inc.
(NASDAQ: RBBN), a publicly traded software, analytics and cloud solutions provider for communications services, which was created from
the merger of Genband US LLC (&#8220;<span style="background-color: white">GENBAND&#8482;&#8221;)</span>, a provider of carrier and enterprise
network transformation and real-time communications solutions, and <span style="background-color: white">Sonus Networks,&#160;Inc. (NASDAQ:
SONS), a publicly traded cloud-based communications distributor that offers mobile network operation and Microsoft solutions</span>. Prior
to his role with Ribbon Communications Inc., he served as an Executive Vice President of Global Sales and Marketing at <span style="background-color: white">GENBAND&#8482;</span>
from January&#160;2016 to December&#160;2017 and as the Chief Marketing Officer and Executive Vice President from March&#160;2015. Mr.&#160;Joggerst
has more than 25 years of experience in various roles in the technology, software, marketing, and telecommunications sectors. Mr.&#160;Joggerst
holds a B.S. in Foreign Service from Georgetown University, with a concentration in international commerce and finance.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><span style="text-decoration: underline">Mark J. Bonney</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Mark J. Bonney has been designated as a Class&#160;II director to serve
until the Company&#8217;s 2025 annual meeting of the stockholders or until his successor has been duly elected and qualified, or until
his earlier death, removal or resignation, and as the Chair of the Audit Committee. The Board has determined that Mr.&#160;Bonney is an
independent director as defined in the NASDAQ Stock Market listing standards and applicable SEC rules&#160;and regulations.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Mark J. Bonney<span style="background-color: white">&#160;currently
serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of On Board Advisors, LLC, a financial and strategic advisory firm. Mr.&#160;Bonney previously
served on the board of directors of Zix Corporation, a then-publicly traded provider of cloud email security solutions, from January&#160;2013
until its merger in December&#160;2021. Mr.&#160;Bonney also previously served as a director of SeaChange International,&#160;Inc. (NASDAQ:SEAC),
a publicly traded provider of end-to-end video delivery and management software solutions for cable wireless, OTT and other content providing
enterprises, from August&#160;2017 through December&#160;2019, including as Executive Chair and principal executive officer from April&#160;2019
through October&#160;2019, and Independent Chairman from October&#160;2019 through December&#160;2019. From May&#160;2018 until its merger
in April&#160;2019, he served as President and Chief Executive Officer and a director of RhythmOne plc (LON:RTHM) (previously known as
Blinkx and also known as RhythmOne Group), an online publicly traded provider of multi-screen digital advertising company, where he also
served as the Interim Chief Financial Officer from February&#160;2019 to April&#160;2019. Prior to that, Mr.&#160;Bonney served as President
and Chief Executive Officer of MRV Communications,&#160;Inc. (NASDAQ:MRVC), a publicly traded supplier of network equipment to the telecommunications
industry, from December&#160;2014 until its sale in August&#160;2017 and as a director of MRV Communications,&#160;Inc. from April&#160;2013
to August&#160;2017. Mr.&#160;Bonney previously served as a director of Sigma Designs,&#160;Inc., a provider of system-on-a-chip semiconductor
solutions for smart homes, from August&#160;2012 through August&#160;2015; Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Direct
Brands,&#160;Inc., a direct to consumer media company, from 2010 to 2012; vice president and general manager of the Authentication Solutions
Group of JDS Uniphase&#160;Corporation (&#8220;JDSU&#8221;), an optical technologies and telecommunications firm, from 2008 to 2010; and
as a director from 2003 until 2005, and Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer from 2005 to 2008, of American Bank Note
Holographics,&#160;Inc., an optical security device company, which was acquired by JDSU. Mr.&#160;Bonney has also previously held executive
roles with technology companies, including President, Chief Operating Officer and a director of Axsys Technologies,&#160;Inc., a manufacturer
of components and subsystems for aerospace, defense, data storage, medical and other high technology applications, from 1999 to 2002,
and Chief Financial Officer of Zygo Corporation, a manufacturer of components for semiconductor, data storage and industrial markets,
from 1993 to 1999. Mr.&#160;Bonney holds a B.S. in Business from Central Connecticut State University and an M.B.A. from the University
of Hartford.</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">For their service as non-employee directors, Mr.&#160;Joggerst and

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: right"><B>Exhibit 99.1&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Plug Power Announces Changes to Board of Directors</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>Two prominent leaders in technology transformation,
strategy, and innovation join the Board of Directors to propel the company to the next phase of its growth as a vertically integrated
green hydrogen company</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt"><B>LATHAM, N.Y., July&nbsp;10, 2023
-- </B></FONT><U>Plug Power Inc.</U> (NASDAQ: PLUG), a leading provider of comprehensive hydrogen solutions for the global green hydrogen
economy, today announced changes to its Board of Directors. With strong backgrounds in technology transformation, entrepreneurship, and
innovation, Patrick Joggerst and Mark Bonney join the company&rsquo;s Board effective immediately to help propel Plug to the next phase
of its growth as a vertically integrated green hydrogen company. Additionally, Mr.&nbsp;Bonney will chair Plug&rsquo;s audit committee.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: left">Mr.&nbsp;Joggerst brings more than 40 years of
expertise and experience as an executive-level leader navigating complex transformations. Throughout his career in telecom infrastructure
and services, Mr.&nbsp;Joggerst played a key role in spearheading significant technology transformations, including AT&amp;T&rsquo;s
international connectivity transition from satellite to undersea fiber, the transition from landline to mobile at companies such as PrimeCo,
Telcordia, and Aricent, and the globalization of services and supply chains for numerous companies. He currently serves as Principal
and Founder of J2 Consulting, a management consulting firm focused on organizational transformation and sales acceleration.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: left">Mr.&nbsp;Bonney brings a wealth of experience
in finance, strategy, and executive leadership, having served various executive roles and as a director for several prominent public
companies. He currently holds the position of President and CEO at On Board Advisors, LLC, a renowned financial and strategic advisory
firm. Mr.&nbsp;Bonney's expertise has proven invaluable in driving growth and delivering strategic insights to organizations across industries.
Mr.&nbsp;Bonney currently serves on the board of TileShop Holdings Inc., and has served as a director and, or, held executive positions
at esteemed companies such as SeaChange International,&nbsp;Inc., MRV Communications,&nbsp;Inc., Sigma Designs,&nbsp;Inc., Direct Brands,&nbsp;Inc.,
JDS Uniphase Corporation, American Bank Note Holographics,&nbsp;Inc., Axsys Technologies,&nbsp;Inc., and Zygo Corporation. Throughout
his career, Mr.&nbsp;Bonney has demonstrated exceptional leadership, financial acumen, and strategic vision.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">In addition, Jean Bua and Jonathan Silver stepped down from Plug&rsquo;s
Board of Directors effective immediately. Mr.&nbsp;Silver will assume a new role as consultant to Plug.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: left"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">&ldquo;</FONT>On
behalf of the Plug Power team, we are extremely grateful for the contributions of Jean Bua and Jonathan Silver to the company&rsquo;s
Board, and we are excited to welcome Patrick Joggerst and Mark Bonney to the Board. As we continue to execute on building the preeminent
green hydrogen company, they will add critical new dimensions to our Board&rsquo;s expertise in the areas of technology transformation,
entrepreneurship, and innovation,&rdquo; said Andy Marsh, Plug&rsquo;s CEO.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>About Plug</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">Plug is building an end-to-end green
hydrogen ecosystem, from production, storage and delivery to energy generation, to help its customers meet their business goals and decarbonize
the economy. In creating the first commercially viable market for hydrogen fuel cell technology, the company has deployed more than 60,000
fuel cell systems and over 180 fueling stations, more than anyone else in the world, and is the largest buyer of liquid hydrogen. With
plans to build and operate a green hydrogen highway across North America and Europe, Plug is operating a state-of-the-art Gigafactory
to produce electrolyzers and fuel cells, and is commissioning multiple green hydrogen production plants that will yield 500 tons of liquid
green hydrogen daily by year end 2025. Plug will deliver its green hydrogen solutions directly to its customers and through joint venture
partners into multiple environments, including material handling, e-mobility, power generation, and industrial applications. For more
information, visit </FONT><U>www.plugpower.com</U>.</P>
