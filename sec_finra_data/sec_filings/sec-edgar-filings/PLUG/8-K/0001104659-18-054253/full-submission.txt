<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 8.01 Other Events</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On August&nbsp;28, 2018, a lawsuit was filed on behalf of eight individuals against Plug Power Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) and five corporate co-defendants in the 9</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">th</font>&#160;Judicial District Court, Rapides Parish, Louisiana.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The lawsuit relates to the previously disclosed May&nbsp;2018 accident involving a forklift powered by a Plug Power fuel cell at a Procter&nbsp;&amp; Gamble facility in Louisiana.&#160; The lawsuit alleges claims against the Company and the co-defendants for defect in construction and/or composition, design defect, inadequate warning, breach of express warranty and negligence. The lawsuit claims an unspecified amount of damages for wrongful death and personal injuries, among other damages.&#160; The Company intends to vigorously defend the litigation.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Given the early stage of this matter, the Company is unable to determine the likelihood of an adverse outcome.&#160; However, the Company does not expect the lawsuit to have a material impact on the Company&#146;s financial position, liquidity or results of operations, or to otherwise have a material adverse effect on the Company.</font></p>
