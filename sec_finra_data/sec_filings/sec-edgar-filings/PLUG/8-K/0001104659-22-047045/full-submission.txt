<P STYLE="margin: 0; text-align: right"><B>Exhibit 99.1</B></P>

<P STYLE="margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Plug Supplies Walmart with Green Hydrogen to
Fuel Retailer&rsquo;s Fleet of Material Handling Lift Trucks</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><I>Liquid green hydrogen to fuel up to 9,500 lift
trucks across Walmart distribution and fulfillment centers, supporting the retailer&rsquo;s goal of a zero emissions future by 2040</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt"><B>LATHAM, N.Y., Apr.&nbsp;19, 2022
</B></FONT>-- <U>Plug Power Inc</U>.(NASDAQ: PLUG), a leading provider of turnkey hydrogen solutions for the global green hydrogen economy,
today announced an agreement with <U>Walmart Inc</U>. (NYSE: WMT) for an option to deliver up to 20 tons per day of liquid green hydrogen
to power material handling lift trucks across Walmart distribution and fulfillment centers in the U.S., marking an additional step the
retailer is taking to incorporate alternative zero-carbon energy sources throughout Walmart&rsquo;s supply chain. This is one of the
first green hydrogen supply contracts for Plug, validating the company&rsquo;s multi-year investment in its green hydrogen network.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;Walmart has been an early adopter of innovative hydrogen and
fuel cell technology for over a decade, and our hydrogen-powered solutions offer a tool to enhance productivity improvements for Walmart&rsquo;s
operations,&rdquo; said Andy Marsh, CEO of Plug. &ldquo;Now our green hydrogen solutions will provide Walmart with the ability to achieve
significant carbon reduction. We are honored to expand our relationship with Walmart and realize our shared vision for a green hydrogen
future.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">Green hydrogen is produced through the
electrolysis of water with electricity generated from zero-carbon sources and only harmless oxygen is emitted during the process, making
it a clean, secure and affordable energy solution. Through Plug&rsquo;s investment in its vertically integrated green hydrogen ecosystem,
the company will deliver green hydrogen to Walmart facilities using a fleet of liquid transport Plug has put into service using capabilities
gained from its recent </FONT><U>acquisition of Applied Cryo Technologies</U>.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Walmart has worked with Plug Power to adopt and expand hydrogen fuel
cells throughout its facilities for over a decade, beginning with a 50-fleet pilot in 2012 and expanding to a fleet of 9,500 and growing.
Now, the retailer is continuing to decarbonize its operations by striving to incorporate the use of green hydrogen throughout its facilities,
in an effort to pursue lift truck operations that are efficient, clean, quiet and most importantly, more sustainable.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;Hydrogen is critical to helping us power a more sustainable
supply chain and Plug Power&rsquo;s hydrogen solutions allow us to continue making progress,&rdquo; said Jeff Smith, Senior Director of
Supply Chain Maintenance Services. &ldquo;Sourcing green hydrogen can help bring Walmart closer to reaching our goal of zero emissions
by 2040.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Over the last several years, Plug has executed on a vision for a network
of green hydrogen production facilities across the globe to meet the demand for decarbonization of the economy and address the urgent
challenge of climate change. The company is targeting 70 TPD of green hydrogen production by end of 2022 and remains on track for 500
TPD of production in North America by 2025 and 1,000 TPD on a global basis by 2028.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>About Plug</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt">Plug is building an end-to-end green
hydrogen ecosystem, from production, storage and delivery to energy generation, to help its customers meet their business goals and decarbonize
the economy. In creating the first commercially viable market for hydrogen fuel cell technology, the company has deployed more than 50,000
fuel cell systems and over 165 fueling stations, more than anyone else in the world, and is the largest buyer of liquid hydrogen. With
plans to build and operate a green hydrogen highway across North America and Europe, Plug is building a state-of-the-art Gigafactory
to produce electrolyzers and fuel cells and multiple green hydrogen production plants that will yield 500 tons of liquid green hydrogen
daily by 2025. Plug will deliver its green hydrogen solutions directly to its customers and through joint venture partners into multiple
environments, including material handling, e-mobility, power generation, and industrial applications. For more information, visit </FONT><U>www.plugpower.com</U>.</P>
