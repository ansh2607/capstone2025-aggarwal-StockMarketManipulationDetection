&#8220;Company&#8221;) issued a press release announcing that it entered into an agreement with FreezPak Logistics, a leading third party
food logistics company (&#8220;FreezPak&#8221;), pursuant to which the Company will provide fuel cells, hydrogen storage and fueling infrastructure
to nine FreezPak sites and nearly 400 lift trucks. A copy of the press release is furnished herewith as Exhibit&#160;99.1.</p>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt"><I>FreezPak will
