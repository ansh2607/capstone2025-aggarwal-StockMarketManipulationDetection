<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election
of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On May 10, 2023, Lucas P. Schneider, a Class III director on the Board
of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of Plug Power, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), and a member of the Board&#8217;s Corporate Governance
and Nominating Committee, notified the Company of his decision not to stand for reelection at the Company&#8217;s 2023 annual meeting
of stockholders (the &#8220;Annual Meeting&#8221;). Mr. Schneider will continue to serve as a director and as a member of the Board&#8217;s
Corporate Governance and Nominating Committee through the end of his current term, which ends on the date of the Annual Meeting. Mr. Schneider
has served on the Board since March 2017. Mr. Schneider&#8217;s decision not to stand for reelection is not the result of a disagreement
