<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman; " ALIGN="left"><B>Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement </B></P></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On January&nbsp;24, 2020, Tilray, Inc. (&#147;Tilray&#148;) entered into (i)&nbsp;an Amended and Restated Profit Participation Agreement (the &#147;A&amp;R
Profit Participation Agreement&#148;) with&nbsp;ABG&nbsp;Intermediate Holdings 2, LLC (&#147;ABG&#148;), which amended and restated in its entirety the Profit Participation Agreement, dated January&nbsp;14, 2019, by and between Tilray and ABG (the
&#147;Original Profit Participation Agreement&#148;) and (ii)&nbsp;the First Amendment to Payment Agreement with ABG (the &#147;Payment Agreement Amendment&#148;), which amends the Payment Agreement, dated January&nbsp;14, 2019, by and between
Tilray and ABG (the &#147;Original Payment Agreement&#148;). The Original Profit Participation Agreement and the Original Payment Agreement were previously disclosed on Tilray&#146;s Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K</FONT> filed on
January&nbsp;14, 2019 with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Pursuant to the A&amp;R Profit Participation Agreement and the Payment Agreement
Amendment, Tilray and ABG agreed that Tilray will no longer have any obligation to pay the additional consideration with an aggregate value of $83,333,333 in cash or in shares of Tilray&#146;s Class&nbsp;2 Common Stock, which were previously subject
to certain triggers pertaining to the regulatory status of tetrahydrocannibinol in the United States or the receipt of $5,000,000 in distributions to Tilray from the Original Profit Participation Agreement derived from cannabis products that contain
tetrahydrocannibinol outside of the United States, in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in, the Original Payment Agreement. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In addition,
as previously disclosed, under the Original Profit Participation Agreement, Tilray purchased from&nbsp;ABG&nbsp;participation rights in up to 49% of&nbsp;the net (i.e., post-expense) royalties from cannabis products bearing certain brands within
the&nbsp;ABG&nbsp;portfolio (ratably based on the portion of the aggregate amount of consideration received by&nbsp;ABG) that&nbsp;ABG&nbsp;receives from the exploitation of certain&nbsp;ABG&nbsp;brands in connection with the development, marketing
and sale of cannabis-related products. Pursuant to the A&amp;R Profit Participation Agreement, beginning January&nbsp;1, 2020 through December&nbsp;31, 2028, Tilray agreed that it will not be entitled to any participation rights unless and until
such participation rights payable to Tilray with respect to each contract year exceeds $10,000,000 (the &#147;Participation Rights Threshold&#148;), and in the event the Participation Rights Threshold is achieved, Tilray will be entitled to its 49%
participation right for the contract year for all amounts in excess of the Participation Rights Threshold. In addition, pursuant to the A&amp;R Profit Participation Agreement, Tilray agreed, beginning January&nbsp;1, 2020 through December&nbsp;31,
2028, Tilray will not be entitled to any guaranteed minimum participation rights with respect to each contract year, other than amounts payable by ABG for 2019 in the amount of $1,666,667, which is payable by ABG on or before February&nbsp;15, 2020.
</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing descriptions of the transactions contemplated by the A&amp;R Profit Participation Agreement and the Payment Agreement Amendment do not
purport to be complete and are subject to, and are qualified in their entirety by, the full text of the A&amp;R Profit Participation Agreement and the Payment Agreement Amendment, which will be filed as exhibits to Tilray&#146;s Annual Report on
Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">10-K</FONT> for the year ended December&nbsp;31, 2019. </P>
