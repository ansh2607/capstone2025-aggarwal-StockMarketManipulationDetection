<td style="vertical-align:top"><p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;text-align:justify">(e)&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;As reported by Tilray, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) in its Current Report on Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">8-K</span> dated October&#160;15, 2020, Edward Wood Pastorius, Jr. stepped down as Chief Revenue Officer of the Company on October&#160;8, 2020. Mr.&#160;Pastorius&#8217;s last day of employment with the Company will be November&#160;15, 2020 (the &#8220;Termination Date&#8221;).</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;text-align:justify">On October&#160;21, 2020 (the &#8220;Effective Date&#8221;), the Company entered into a separation agreement and complete release with Mr.&#160;Pastorius (the &#8220;Separation Agreement&#8221;) specifying the terms of Mr.&#160;Pastorius&#8217;s termination of service with the Company.</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;text-align:justify">Pursuant to the Separation Agreement, Mr.&#160;Pastorius will receive payment of Mr.&#160;Pastorius&#8217;s base salary and continued vesting of options and restricted stock units (&#8220;RSUs&#8221;) through the Termination Date.
