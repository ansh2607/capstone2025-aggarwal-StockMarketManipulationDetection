  <div><span style="font-weight: bold;"> Item 3.02</span>
    <div style="margin: -12pt 0px 0px; text-indent: 72pt;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Unregistered Sale of Equity Securities</span></div>

    <div><span style="font-weight: bold;"> <br />
      </span></div>

    <div>
      <div>On December 7, 2021, Tilray, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221; or &#8220;Tilray&#8221;) entered into a Business Combination Agreement (&#8220;BCA&#8221;) pursuant to which Tilray completed the acquisition of Double Diamond Distillery LLC (d/b/a Breckenridge
        Distillery, &#8220;DDD&#8221;), a Colorado limited liability company (the &#8220;Acquisition&#8221;).</div>

      <div><br />
      </div>

      <div>As consideration for the Acquisition, the Company paid a purchase price in an aggregate amount equal to $102.9 million, which purchase price was satisfied through the issuance of 11,245,511 of Tilray&#8217;s Class 2 common shares to the selling unit
        holders of DDD (the &#8220;Consideration Shares&#8221;). The Consideration Shares were offered and sold in reliance upon an exemption from registration pursuant to Section 4(a)(2) under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, for the offer and sale of
        securities not involving a public offering.</div>

      <div><br />
      </div>

      <div>No underwriter participated in the offer and sale of the Consideration Shares, and no commission or other remuneration was paid or given directly or indirectly in connection therewith.</div>

    <div style="text-align: center; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">TILRAY STRENGTHENS STRATEGIC POSITION IN THE U.S. WITH ACQUISITION OF BRECKENRIDGE DISTILLERY</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: center; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;"><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-weight: bold;">Accretive Acquisition</font>&#160;<font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-weight: bold;">of Award-Winning Producer of Distilled Spirits Expands Tilray&#8217;s U.S. Offerings into the On-Trend Spirits Category</font></div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: center; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Tilray&#8217;s Diversified, Growing CPG Portfolio in the U.S. Delivers Revenue and EBITDA and Establishes Robust Infrastructure Upon Cannabis
      Legalization</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;"><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-weight: bold;">NEW YORK &#8211; DECEMBER 8, 2021 --</font> Tilray, Inc.
      (&#8220;Tilray&#8221; or the &#8220;Company&#8221;) (NASDAQ | TSX: TLRY), a leading global cannabis-lifestyle and consumer packaged goods company inspiring and empowering the worldwide community to live their very best life, <font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; color: #0A0A0A;">today announced the strategic acquisition of Breckenridge Distillery, a leading distilled spirits platform located in Breckenridge, Colorado widely-known for its award-winning bourbon whiskey
        collection and innovative craft spirits portfolio.&#160; Tilray expects the acquisition to be immediately accretive to EBITDA.</font></div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; color: #323232; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">Irwin D. Simon, Chairman and CEO, said, "Tilray&#8217;s strength lies in our ability to identify and significantly expand leading CPG lifestyle
        brands that resonate powerfully with consumers.&#160; Breckenridge Distillery is an iconic addition to our platform in this respect based on its portfolio of award-winning spirits, passionate consumer engagement, and a strong sales and distribution
        network.&#160; We see tremendous potential for Breckenridge and our existing SweetWater brand to complement each other, expanding their respective reach and driving further profitable growth in our beverage alcohol segment.&#8221;</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;"><font style="color: #201F1E; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">Mr. Simon continued, &#8220;More generally, the Breckenridge
          Distillery transaction is consistent with Tilray&#8217;s strategy of leveraging our growing portfolio of U.S. CPG brands to launch THC-based product adjacencies upon federal legalization in the U.S.&#160; These significant, diversified revenue streams are
          key to </font><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; color: #323232;">delivering on our ultimate goal of industry leadership with $4 billion in revenue by the end of fiscal year 2024.&#8221;</font></div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">Bryan Nolt, Breckenridge Distillery&#8217;s Founder and Chief Executive Officer, added, &#8220;We are excited to join Tilray and drive revenue growth
      as part of its global and leading CPG and cannabis-lifestyle platform.&#160; The award-winning spirits that have driven our success will unquestionably benefit from access to Tilray&#8217;s global distribution network and opportunities to expand into cannabis
      and edible-related products in the U.S.&#8221;</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;"><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; color: #323232;">Founded in 2008 by Mr. Nolt, the Breckenridge Distillery is
        most widely known for its blended bourbon whiskey, a high-rye mash American-style whiskey, and a collection of artisanal spirits offering that brings to life the best that Colorado has to offer.&#160; Breckenridge Distillery&#8217;s consumer engagement
        strategy is defined and differentiated through unique experiential marketing and highly-effective social media outreach and includes </font><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; color: #000000;">a lucrative
        and exclusive partnership as the hometown Bourbon of the Denver Broncos.&#160; Among other accolades, Breckenridge&#8217;s blended bourbon whiskey is a 4x winner of Best American Blended Whiskey from the World Whiskies Awards. In 2018, Breckenridge was
        awarded the prestigious </font><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-style: italic; color: #000000;">Icons of Whisky award for Brand Innovator of the Year </font><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; color: #000000;">by Whisky Magazine.</font></div>

      Franklin.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Strategic and Financial Benefits</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">In addition to acquiring a strong brand and accretive business, this strategic acquisition delivers additional scale in the beverage
      alcohol categories and further positions Tilray with infrastructure and a larger footprint in the U.S. market upon federal cannabis legalization.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">Tilray further believes the acquisition will provide several financial and strategic benefits, including:</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Joins Sweetwater to Further Diversify and Grow Tilray&#8217;s Beverage Alcohol Business:</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">Breckenridge Distillery joins SweetWater Brewing Company as the cornerstones of Tilray&#8217;s beverage alcohol segment and further diversifies
      the company&#8217;s net revenue mix.&#160; Breckenridge Distillery is expected to be immediately margin accretive with Breckenridge generating adjusted EBITDA margins of approximately 25 percent.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Provides for Additional Significant Innovation Opportunities while Expanding Tilray&#8217;s Addressable Market in the U.S.:</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">When federally permissible, Tilray believes the acquisition of Breckenridge Distillery will enable Tilray to commercialize new and
      innovative products through the development of non-alcoholic distilled spirits, including bourbon whisky, that is infused with cannabis.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Opportunity for Accelerated Growth of Breckenridge Distillery:</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: justify; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt;">With more than 85% of its revenue generated in Colorado, Breckenridge Distillery has enormous potential to expand its customer base and
      grow throughout the U.S. as a true national brand. To that end, Tilray intends to leverage SweetWater&#8217;s existing nationwide infrastructure to accelerate Breckenridge Distillery&#8217;s and create new, greatly-expanded consumer awareness and product
      adoption.</div>
