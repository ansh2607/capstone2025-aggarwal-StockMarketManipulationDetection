<p style="text-align:justify;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:11pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align:justify;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;text-indent:0%;color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Calibri;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">PALO ALTO, Calif., August 11, 2020 &#8211; Tesla, Inc. (&#8220;Tesla&#8221;) announced today that the Board of Directors has approved and declared a five-for-one split of Tesla&#8217;s common stock in the form of a stock dividend to make stock ownership more accessible to employees and investors. Each stockholder of record on August 21, 2020 will receive a dividend of four additional shares of common stock for each then-held share, to be distributed after close of trading on August 28, 2020. Trading will begin on a stock split-adjusted basis on August 31, 2020. </p>
