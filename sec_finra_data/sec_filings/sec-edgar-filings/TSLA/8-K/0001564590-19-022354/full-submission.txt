<p style="margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:10pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:10pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">On June 11, 2019, the Tesla, Inc. 2019 Equity Incentive Plan (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">2019 Plan</font>&#8221;) was approved by Tesla&#8217;s stockholders at Tesla&#8217;s 2019 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Annual Meeting</font>&#8221;), and the Tesla, Inc. 2010 Equity Incentive Plan (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">2010 Plan</font>&#8221;) simultaneously terminated. The 2019 Plan, which was previously adopted by Tesla&#8217;s Board of Directors (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Board</font>&#8221;) subject to the approval of Tesla&#8217;s stockholders, allows Tesla to grant equity awards thereunder with respect to (i) a maximum of 12,500,000 shares of Tesla&#8217;s common stock (&#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Common Stock</font>&#8221;), plus (ii) such number of shares issued or subject to awards granted under the 2010 Plan prior to its termination that expire, terminate, or are forfeited or repurchased due to failure to vest. Awards under the 2019 Plan may be granted to Tesla&#8217;s eligible employees, directors or consultants, and the 2019 Plan will be administered by the Board, its Compensation Committee or any other committee designated by the Board pursuant to the terms of the 2019 Plan.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:10pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:10pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">The foregoing description of the 2019 Plan is qualified by reference to the 2019 Plan and forms of stock option and restricted stock unit award agreements thereunder, which are filed as Exhibits 10.1, 10.2 and 10.3 hereto, respectively, and incorporated by reference herein. </p>

<p style="margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:10pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-size:10pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">At the Annual Meeting, the Tesla, Inc. 2019 Employee Stock Purchase Plan (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">2019 ESPP</font>&#8221;) was approved by Tesla&#8217;s stockholders. Pursuant to the 2019 ESPP, which was previously adopted by the Board subject to the approval of Tesla&#8217;s stockholders, Tesla&#8217;s eligible employees will be permitted to purchase up to a maximum of 7,500,000 aggregate shares of Common Stock through payroll deductions at the end of consecutive, non-overlapping six-month offering periods, the first of which is expected to commence in September 2019. The 2019 ESPP will be administered by the Board, its Compensation Committee or any other committee designated by the Board pursuant to the terms of the 2019 ESPP.</p>
