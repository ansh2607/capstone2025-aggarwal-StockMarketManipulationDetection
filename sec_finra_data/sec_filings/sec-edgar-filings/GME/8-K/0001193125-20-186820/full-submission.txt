<td style="width:10%;vertical-align:top;white-space:nowrap;">Item&#160;5.02 </td>
<td style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</td> </tr> </table> <p style="font-family:Times New Roman;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:6pt">On June&#160;29, 2020, GameStop Corp. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) announced the appointment of Diana Saadeh-Jajeh, age 50, as the Company&#8217;s Senior Vice President and Chief Accounting Officer, effective June&#160;29, 2020. Since November 2018, Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh has served as Vice President, Global Finance Operations&#160;&#038; Transformation of JUUL Labs, Inc. a global electronic cigarette company. From August 2016 to November 2018, Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh served as Senior Business Lead consultant, <span style="white-space:nowrap">Acquire-to-Retire</span> Transformation for Equinix, Inc., a publicly traded multinational company specializing in internet connection and data centers. From January 2015 to April 2016, Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh served as Vice President and Global Corporate Controller at e.l.f. Beauty, Inc., an American cosmetics company. Previously Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh served in various accounting, finance and financial reporting positions for Visa Inc. from 1997 to 2015. Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh is a Certified Public Accountant. </p> <p style="font-family:Times New Roman;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:12pt">Upon assumption by Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh of her position as Senior Vice President and Chief Accounting Officer, James A. Bell, the Company&#8217;s Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, who had assumed the position of the Company&#8217;s principal accounting officer on May&#160;15, 2020, will cease to be the Company&#8217;s principal accounting officer and will continue in his position as the Company&#8217;s Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer. </p> <p style="font-family:Times New Roman;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:12pt">Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh entered into a letter agreement with the Company on June 23, 2020 describing the basic terms of her employment. The letter provides that Ms.&#160;Saadeh-Jajeh&#8217;s starting annual salary will be $375,000 and her target annual bonus opportunity will be equal to 60% of her annual salary.
